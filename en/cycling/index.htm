Cycling

Bike rental & private tour

Explore the delights near the hotel by cycling with our renowned service, Medfeel® Premium Cycling. Enjoy access to top-notch muscle or electric bikes and knowledgeable local guides who will lead you on carefully curated routes to uncover the natural and cultural treasures of Mallorca.

Cycling dossier

The best way to explore the island...

Explore the heart of Mallorca

Cross towns, vineyards and well-known cycling routes. Stop to have coffee accompanied by a Mallorcan "coca de patata" or "llonguet". Recover your strength and continue along this wonderful route through the heart of the island.

Cure Sanctuary Challenge

Enjoy stunning views as you travel to the top of Puig de Randa. There the Sanctuary built in the 15th century awaits you, which will add a historical dimension to your tour.
