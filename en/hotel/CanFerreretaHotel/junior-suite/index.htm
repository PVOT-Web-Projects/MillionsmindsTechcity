Junior Suite

A CHARM OF A ROOM

Spacious and wonderfully bright suites measuring 45 m2, some with extremely high ceilings. Centuries-old stone arches, vaulted ceilings, desks, double wardrobe, bench or reading chairs. Large bathrooms, two with a free-standing Origin oriental bathtub and shower cubicle.

FEATURES

WHY CHOOSE THE JUNIOR SUITE?

  • Surface area of 45 m2

  • For up to 2 people

  • King-size bed

  • Shower cubicle

  • Free-standing bathtub

  • Garden Views & Can Ferrereta Street
