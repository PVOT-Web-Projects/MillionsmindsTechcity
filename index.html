What is Million Minds
Tech City?

Picture a world where life seamlessly merges with work. Crafted by the visionary minds at GHCL, a space that isn’t bound by conventional boundaries.

Imagine a fusion of retail therapy, residential bliss, and commercial prowess.

Spread over 65 Acers, Million Minds Tech City is a first of its kind integrated development in Ahmedabad, at par with the best IT parks and residences in the country comprising offices, Co- working, Residential, Co- living, Hotel, Club and Neighbourhood mall.

With an infrastructure of international standards, this is the perfect platform for a global business set-up. It is a remarkable confluence of IT, Innovation & Sustainable Development set to attract attention worldwide.

GHCL

A name you can trust

Ganesh Housing Corporation Limited, Real estate Developers that combines excellence and innovation. With over 60 years of experience, we have become pioneers in the Gujarat real estate sector, transforming the urban landscape with iconic buildings and cutting- edge spaces that has been dedicated to excellence and innovation.

Our impressive portfolio comprises 22 million square feet of developed space within the city and an additional 35 million square feet currently under development, demonstrating our unwavering commitment to elevating living and working experiences to unparalleled heights.

We are now adding yet another landmark to the city, with an aim to make Million Minds Tech City the Silicon Valley of Western India, Get ready to witness a new era of digitization in Ahmedabad.

Tishman Speyer

From New York to Ahmedabad

Tishman Speyer has built over 180 assets worldwide on a landscape of 84 Mn Sq feet. At Tishman Speyer, Pioneers in the art of creating vibrant destinations, and dynamic environments that inspire innovation and connect communities.

Since 1978, they have acquired, managed or developed over 270 properties totalling over 115 million sq. ft. and 92,000 residential units representing a portfolio of over US$ 76 billion across the U.S., Europe, Latin America and Asia.

Tishman Speyer specializes in repositioning, developing and redeveloping premier, Grade-A office, residential and mixed-use properties located in Tier-I markets worldwide.
